Cookie Policy

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our website.

2. How We Use Cookies

Apex Pest Control uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly and provide basic features
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Session management cookies
  • Security cookies
  • Load balancing cookies

Analytics and Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve our services:

  • Google Analytics cookies
  • Page view tracking
  • User behavior analysis

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

  • User preference cookies
  • Language preference cookies
  • Chat widget cookies

Targeting and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements relevant to you:

  • Advertising platform cookies
  • Retargeting cookies
  • Social media cookies

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies on your device. These include:

  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis
  • Social Media Platforms: For social sharing features
  • Advertising Networks: For delivering targeted advertisements

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie policies.

5. How Long Do Cookies Last

Cookies can be either session cookies or persistent cookies:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Our Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • View detailed information about the cookies we use

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly. This could affect:

  • Your ability to log in or maintain your session
  • Website personalization features
  • Form submission functionality
  • Our ability to remember your preferences

8. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. Our website does not currently respond to DNT signals. However, you can use the cookie settings in your browser to manage tracking.

9. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a new effective date.
